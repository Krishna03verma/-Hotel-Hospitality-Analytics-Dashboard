## 📌 Overview

The Hotel & Hospitality Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution designed to analyze hotel booking performance, revenue generation, customer behavior, cancellation trends, and geographical booking distribution.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of hotel operations by transforming booking data into actionable insights, helping stakeholders improve occupancy, reduce cancellations, optimize revenue, and enhance customer satisfaction.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Hotels receive bookings from various customer segments, countries, and booking channels. Managing cancellations, maximizing revenue, understanding customer behavior, and identifying high-performing markets are critical challenges for the hospitality industry.

Without proper analysis, businesses may struggle to:

- Reduce booking cancellations.
- Improve occupancy rates.
- Understand customer preferences.
- Identify profitable market segments.
- Optimize revenue generation.
- Track geographical demand patterns.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to develop an interactive Power BI dashboard that:

- Monitors hotel booking performance.
- Analyzes cancellation trends and risk factors.
- Evaluates customer behavior and booking patterns.
- Tracks revenue performance across hotels and market segments.
- Identifies top-performing countries and customer segments.
- Supports strategic decision-making through data-driven insights.

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Total bookings exceeded 119K across the analysis period.
- Revenue generated reached 42.72M.
- The overall cancellation rate was 37.04%, indicating a significant impact on hotel operations.
- City Hotels recorded higher booking volumes and higher cancellation rates compared to Resort Hotels.
- Online TA emerged as the highest revenue-generating and most cancellation-prone market segment.
- August recorded the highest revenue and booking activity.
- Portugal contributed the highest number of bookings and revenue.
- Transient customers represented the largest customer segment.
- Repeat guests accounted for a relatively small percentage of total customers.
- Switzerland recorded the highest ADR among analyzed countries.
